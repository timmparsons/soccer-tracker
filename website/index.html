FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything players and parents ask about Master Touch.

What is Master Touch?

Master Touch is a free soccer training app for youth players. It tracks touches on the ball, juggling records, and training sessions, then turns consistent practice into streaks, daily challenges, and team leaderboards so kids stay motivated to train every day. Learn more

Is Master Touch free?

Yes. Master Touch is free to download on the App Store. Most competing soccer training apps charge $5–$38 per month; Master Touch’s touch tracking, streaks, challenges, and leaderboards are free. Learn more

How do you track soccer touches with Master Touch?

Open the Train tab, then either log a session after you finish or use the built-in timer while you train. Every touch counts toward your daily goal, weekly totals, and your team leaderboard position. Learn more

How many touches a day should a youth soccer player get?

A common benchmark in youth soccer is 1,000 touches a day to sharpen skills, with ambitious players aiming for 2,500 or more. Master Touch lets players set a daily touch goal and shows a progress ring so they always know how close they are. Learn more

What soccer drills can kids do at home with Master Touch?

Master Touch includes a drill library with video demos plus a new challenge every day, like Foot Catches in 3 minutes. All drills are designed for small spaces: a backyard, driveway, or garage. Learn more

Does Master Touch track juggling?

Yes. Master Touch has a dedicated juggling leaderboard alongside the touches leaderboard, so players can log juggling records and compete with teammates today, this week, or all time. Learn more

What age is Master Touch best for?

Master Touch is designed for youth soccer players and their parents. Younger kids can train with a parent logging sessions, while older players run their own streaks, challenges, and leaderboard battles. Learn more

How does Master Touch keep kids motivated to practice soccer?

Day streaks, daily challenges with difficulty tiers, team leaderboards with podiums and medals, and Coach Vinnie, the in-app coach who delivers daily motivation. Practice becomes a game kids want to win. Learn more

Does Master Touch have a free training program?

Yes. The 30-Day Touch Challenge is a free day-by-day program published on this site: roughly 25,000 touches over 30 days, starting at 300 touches a day and building to the classic 1,000-a-day habit. Start the challenge
