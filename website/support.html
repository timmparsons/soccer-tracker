Help & Contact

About the app

Master Touch is a soccer training app designed to help youth players improve their technical skills through structured challenges and team-based rankings.

Need help?

If you are experiencing issues or have questions, please contact us:

Email: tim@mastertouch.app

We typically respond within 24–48 hours.

Frequently asked questions

How do I join a team?

Enter the team code provided by your coach during sign up.

Who can see my rankings?

Rankings are only visible to players within the same team.

How do I reset my password?

Use the password reset link on the login screen.

Who should use this app?

Master Touch is designed for team use under coach and parent supervision.
