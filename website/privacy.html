Master Touch Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Master Touch respects your privacy.

Microphone access

Our app may request access to your device’s microphone solely to enable in-app audio features such as sound effects or training feedback. Audio is not recorded, stored, or transmitted off your device.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal audio data.

Your data

User data such as training activity and progress is stored securely and is only used to provide app functionality.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
